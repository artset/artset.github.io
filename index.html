Hello, I'm Katherine! I'm fascinated by the connections between the art of creating and curating -- which I explore through illustration, writing, and coding. Currently, I am trying to master the art of sprezzatura, reading a handful of books, and learning French. You will most likely catch me camping out in some library or on the L, probably going anywhere and everywhere. I collect color palettes (#7EBDC2, #FFE5BF, $FF77272), words(rigmarole, stochastic, mulch), and good music in peculiar key signatures (6/4, 5/8 + 7/8, 6/8 + 9/8 ). Outside of that, I am a fan of fine pens, magical surrealism, and that moment when the words "all test passed" appear on my screen.
