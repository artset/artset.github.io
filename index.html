Hi there! I'm a computer science undergrad at Brown University, where I'm excited about every facet of creation, from writing computationally elegant solutions to illustrating poignant stories. Currently, I'm taking a lot of math classes as a study in human empathy, learning how to read Mandarin, and constantly working towards the art of sprezzatura.

At Brown, I work as a graphic designer for the Brown Center for Students of Color, create layouts for the College Independent, am a part of the STEAM internal team, and am on the Hack@Brown outreach team! When I'm not ranting about a short story I just read, you can typically find me browsing in stacks at the RISD library, sketching, or taking an absurdly long walk.

Beyond my digital adventures and humble code, feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn Behance, and Medium.

This website is built w/ love, set in Tajawal, and hosted on Github.
