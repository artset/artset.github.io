sketchbook

Ongoing. On sketchbook #6.

Watercolor, ink.

As a kid, my stories were always accompanied with elaborate maps of neighborhoods, cities, wheatfield mazes, forests. However, visual art had always been an afterthought to the words I supplied to paper. Then, at the beginning of high school, I bought my first sketchbook to create some architectural studies for a series of vignettes I was writing at the time. From this, I began incorporating sketching into my daily life. Sketchbooks are a delightful way to see one grow. My relationship with each sketchbook is like making a new friend -- each one is unique and has pushed me to grow.
