sky bub

January - February 2019, Design Studio

Arduino, laser cut plywood, wood glue, acrylic, motor, LED lights, motion sensor.

sky bub is an interactive sculpture evoking a sense of play. Inspired by the numerous times that museum security guards have asked me to step away from a painting, sky bub inverts this moment through inviting proximity between the viewer and art. sky bub reacts upon closeness, changing from day to night. At night time, the stars twinkle. One can come close again to turn the scene back to day.
