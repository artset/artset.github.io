Gaggle

Summer 2018, Freelance

XD, Illustrator

View interactive prototype.

Last summer, I was hired by some of my peers to transform their basic wireframes into high fidelity prototypes to help them build their app. In addition, I was involved in iterating upon their wireframes to improve the UX and design complementary graphics to easily explain how to use the app.

The purpose of Gaggle is to crowd source purchasing of groceries and food. For research, I reviewed and noted the design decisions in similar apps such as Postmates, Uber eats, and Lyft. A crucial difference b/w those apps and Gaggle is that Gaggle aims to seamlessly combine the delivery and shopping into a single app.
