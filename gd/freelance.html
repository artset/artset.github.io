Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign.

I've done a variety of graphic design work, whether it a 30+ page education catalog, restaurant menu, works for literary arts magazines and newspapers, or posters for organizations that I strongly believe in. Here is my most recent work in the past 2 years.

Select works from my time at the Brown Center for Students of Color, including a poster for a screenprint & chill event by the Asian American Heritage Series, a dinner by the Black Heritage Series, and the closing end of year part for the BCSC.

I also had the privilege of creating the TWTP (Third World Transition Program. one of Brown's freshman pre-orientations programs) brochure. It's pretty cool because I actually attended it freshman year! See more work I made for the BCSC.

In summer 2019, I was commissioned to design a 30+ page catalog for Baker&Bloom, an education center in Hong Kong. The catalog featured the course schedule, general information about the center, and synopses of each of the classes. I worked remotely, actively communicating with the founder and teachers by creating this catalog over the course of 3 weeks.

For the fall 2019, I will be continuing to do freelance work at the Sarah Doyle Center, as well as managing their newsletter and other odds and ends. Check back as I update this page when I do more work there! :)
