my work

Hello there! I’m a junior at Brown University, where I study computer science and its intersections with visual arts, teaching, and language. If I’m not scribbling in my sketchbook, you can probably find me foraging for plywood at the Brown Design Workshop or brewing a syntactical mess in the Computer Science department. Outside of school, I’m a huge fan of jazz, prose and poetry thick with curling symbolism, and persimmons.

I would love to chat about books, code, and design (among many, many other things -- send recs of the things you love!). Feel free to shoot a message at katherine_sang [at] brown [dot] e-dee-yew.
