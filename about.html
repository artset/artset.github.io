my work

Hi, I'm Katherine! I'm a junior at Brown University, where I study computer science and its intersections with art, writing, and teaching. This semester, I will be working as a monitor in training at the Brown Design Workshop, a publicity coordinator and graphic designer the Sarah Doyle Center for Women & Gender, and finding every opportunity possible to create and grow. I am involved with STEAM, Hack@Brown, and the College Independent, where I do a variety of activities, from teaching workshops to managing Medium pages to designing layouts.

Everything I do is with the goal of bringing life to static elements, whether it be a table given movement through its curves, a short story with a crinkly smile, or an exciting piece of code that runs.
